SCHOLARSHIP OPPORTUNITIES

Access to quality education is important as it gives you the resources to succeed. We compiled a list of scholarships that can help you access quality education or fund your education:

Equity Wings To Fly
(equitygroupfoundation.com)

"The Equity scholarship program was formed to aid high performing children hailing from less fortunate backgrounds. The program beneficiaries are selected from across Kenya’s 47 counties, based on their performance in the Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE) examination."

Beacon Scholars
(beaconscholarship.com)

"The Beacon Scholarship promote access to quality education for financially disadvantaged students who show leadership potential, but do not have sufficient means to pay full fees."

International School of Kenya Scholarship
(isk.ac.ke)

"The ISK Scholarship targets low to middle income Kenyan students who would otherwise be unable to afford our tuition and fees. ISK pays for 100% of their costs in High School, including the IB Diploma program, school fees, admission fees, and bus fees. As long as students maintain good academic performance and behavior, we will invest in their creative, personal and academic development."

Peponi Scholarship
(peponischool.org)

"Peponi School welcomes applications for Scholarships and Exhibitions at 13+ entry and at 16+ entry. The aim is to reward and encourage pupils for excelling either academically or in extra-curricular activities."

St Andrews Turi Scholarship
(standrewsturi.com)

"The true value of a scholarship lies in the extra opportunities that will stretch and challenge your child, both academically and beyond the classroom."

Hillcrest
(hillcrest.ac.ke)

"In line with our vision ‘for every child to achieve personal excellence as a valuable member of our global community’, scholarship awards are intended to recognise the potential for excellence in a range of areas and support those candidates in achieving their very best by providing opportunities they may otherwise not access."
