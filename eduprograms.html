EDUCATIONAL PROGRAMS

As both of the members of Allearn having participated in Educational Programs, this is no new topic to us

Here is a list of our favourite educational programs that are all FREE of charge:

Santa Clara University - Summer Engineering Seminar
(scu.edu)

This is a five-day immersion in July for current high school sophomores (form 2) who are interested in exploring the field of engineering. They offer travel scholarships which cover most if not all expenses to and from Santa Clara.

Techgirls
(techgirlsglobal.org)

This is a program for high-school girls. It is a 25-day experience in the United States in partnership with Virginia Tech University.

Girls Who Code - Self Paced Program
(girlswhocode.com)

Offered by the Girls Who Code organisation, this is a 6 week long program that introduces the basics of programming to girls in and past highschool.

Rusinga Hackathon
(rusinga.ac.ke)

This is a 2 week long programming competition and mentorship program by the Rusinga Schools.

Yale Young African Scholars
(yale.edu)

This is an intensive academic and program for African secondary students who plan to pursue university and want to make meaningful impact on the continent.
